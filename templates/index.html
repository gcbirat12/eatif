Eat If...

Eat If is a user-friendly web application that provides dietary recommendations based on the nutritional content of food, such as calories, protein, carbohydrates, and fat. Using advanced machine learning techniques, the app categorizes food items into balanced, indulgent, or nourishing categories, offering ironic and engaging feedback. Powered by Python and scikit-learn, 'Eat If' leverages pre-trained models to provide quick and accurate recommendations.
